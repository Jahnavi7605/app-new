Welcome to Splash

Splash is a platform where you can explore, customize, and purchase artworks to bring your creative visions to life. With a wide range of artwork categories including watercolour paintings, acrylic paintings, oil paintings, abstract paintings, boho paintings, portrait paintings, and landscape paintings, you'll find something unique to suit your style.

Our website allows you to customize artworks according to your preferences, whether it's adjusting the size, color palette, or style. You can also browse through our collection of just-launched artworks, explore curated gallery walls, or even request personalized art pieces tailored to your specific requirements.

At Splash, we believe in fostering creativity and providing a seamless shopping experience for art enthusiasts and creators alike. Join us on this journey where creativity meets canvas!
